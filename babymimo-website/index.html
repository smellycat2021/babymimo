Features

Everything your growing
family needs

Built for busy parents who don't want to miss a thing.

📸

Moment Logging

Capture milestones, daily activities, firsts, and special memories with photos and videos across multiple categories.

🏆

Milestone Tracking

Browse a curated library of developmental milestones organized by age. Mark each one achieved and attach your favorite photo.

🍼

One-Tap Activity Tracking

Log feeds, sleep, and diaper changes instantly — even from your home screen widget or Siri. Built for sleep-deprived parents.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Sharing

Invite partners, grandparents, or caregivers via a shareable link. Everyone stays in sync through iCloud in real time.

🎬

Memory Videos

Automatically compile milestone photos into a beautiful memory video set to music — saved privately on your device.

📮

Printed Postcards

Mail a real printed postcard to grandparents or loved ones featuring your baby's milestone photo. Delivered to their door.

☁️

Private iCloud Sync

All data lives in your personal iCloud container — not our servers. Your family's memories stay completely private.

🔔

Milestone Reminders

Get notified when upcoming milestones are approaching based on your baby's age, so you never miss a developmental moment.

👶

Multiple Profiles

Track more than one child from a single account. Perfect for growing families.
